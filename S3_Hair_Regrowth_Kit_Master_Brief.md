### 4. How to Use

#### Minoxifin (Topical Solution)
- Apply **1 ml (4 sprays)** on affected areas: temples, hairline, crown
- Massage gently with fingertips for **1–2 minutes**
- Let dry for **10–15 minutes** before styling
- Use **twice daily** — morning and night
- Always apply on **dry, clean scalp** — wet or oily hair significantly reduces absorption

#### Biotin Gummies
- Take **1 gummy daily**
- Any time of day, with or without food
