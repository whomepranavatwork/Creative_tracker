### 4. How to Use

#### Minoxifin Advance (Topical Solution)
- Apply **1 ml** on affected areas: temples, hairline, crown
- Massage gently with fingertips for **1–2 minutes**
- Let dry before styling
- Use **twice daily** — morning and night
- Always apply on a **dry, clean scalp** — wet or oily hair reduces absorption

#### Biotin Gummies
- Take **1 gummy daily**
- Any time of day, with or without food

#### Dermaroller
- Use **once a week** — on a clean, dry scalp
- Roll gently in **three directions**: horizontal, vertical, diagonal
- Apply Minoxifin Advance **immediately after** dermarolling to maximise channel-absorption
- Clean the roller head after each use. Replace every 2–3 months.
